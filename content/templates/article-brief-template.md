## Pre-Publish Checklist

- [ ] Hook grabs attention in first 2 sentences
- [ ] Surat context in first 100 words
- [ ] Every H2 has at least 1 interactive element
- [ ] No generic AI phrases ("delve", "leverage", "landscape")
- [ ] Active voice >90%
- [ ] Primary keyword in: title, H1, first 100 words, 1 H2, conclusion
- [ ] At least 3 Surat location references
- [ ] At least 1 local business example
- [ ] Meta title ≤60 chars, includes "Surat"
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 chars with CTA
- [ ] 3-5 internal links
- [ ] 2-3 external authority links
- [ ] All images have alt text
- [ ] FAQ uses exact question format
- [ ] Conclusion has WhatsApp CTA link
