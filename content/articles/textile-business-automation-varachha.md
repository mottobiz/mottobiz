**The solution:** Auto-respond to common questions (price, stock, MOQ) instantly. You only handle the complex ones.

### Problem 2: Losing Deals to Faster Competitors

**The situation:** A buyer messages 5 traders. The one who responds first gets the deal.

**The solution:** Automated responses hit their phone in under 30 seconds, even when you're busy.

### Problem 3: No System for Follow-ups

**The situation:** You quote a price, the buyer says "I'll think about it," and you never follow up.

**The situation:** You promise fabric you don't have. Or you have fabric but don't know it.

### Problem 5: Price Quote Delays

**The situation:** Every price quote takes 10 minutes of typing. Multiply by 20 inquiries daily.

**The solution:** Instant price quotes based on fabric type and quantity. Your pricing, your rules, zero typing.
